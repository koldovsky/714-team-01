About Us

We are located on 40 acres in Oklahoma, near the Ouachita National Forest with an abundance of trails for our puppies to enjoy. It's a perfect setting to raise our beautiful huskies of different species. We have been breeding AKC Husky Puppies since 2000. We have Champions and Champion bloodlines alike but we're sure that a puppy is a member of a family, first of all, no matter what are its characteristics.

We sell our puppies only to responsible people. Huskies are very different from other dogs. Before deciding to take such a puppy home, please conduct research and learn more about the housing conditions of such dogs to keep them healthy.

Our History

At Happy Husky, we understand that thinking about buying a husky is a HUGE commitment for you and your family. Our kennel started as just one gorgeous husky gifted by David's parents. Now we are a registered AKC breeder with 10 adult huskies and 4-6 puppies for sale every month.

September

2000

While still a schoolboy, David received his first dog - a Siberian Husky female puppy named Akasu. David understood that huskies are love for life.

February

2010

Our kennel name "Happy Husky" was registered and protected by FCI: reg. no. 648/12. That day we became an official American husky breeder!

May

2018

Our husky Sunny won the Top Champion Of The Year 2018 title and became a new USA Show Champion. Meanwhile, by 2018, many dogs from kennel also received their significant awards at various shows.
