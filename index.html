About Us

We are located on 40 acres in Oklahoma, near the Ouachita National Forest with an abundance of trails for our puppies to enjoy. It's a perfect setting to raise our beautiful huskies of different species. We have been breeding AKC Husky Puppies since 2000. We have Champions and Champion bloodlines alike but we're sure that a puppy is a member of a family, first of all, no matter what are its characteristics.



We sell our puppies only to responsible people. Huskies are very different from other dogs. Before deciding to take such a puppy home, please conduct research and learn more about the housing conditions of such dogs to keep them healthy.

FAQ

Be sure to get familiar with all the features of the husky breed well before buying a puppy! If you still have questions about keeping a husky or buying a puppy from our kennel, contact us directly.

What are the height and weight of an adult husky?

The official breed standard (FCI Standard No. 270 of January 24, 2000) provides males' height of 53.5-60 cm, females' height of 50.5-56 cm. Males are rarely heavier than 28 kg (23 kg for females).

How often is the deworming of dogs done?

Worms can be in any dog, regardless of age, breed, and lifestyle. The veterinary service recommends to carry out prevention measures on a quarterly basis, as well as before vaccination and in the fall.

Are huskies suitable for families with children?

Huskies are tender of children, especially if they grow with them. Still, the husky is a large and energetic dog, it can accidentally push a small child, so control the games of your husky and the child.

Is it difficult to care for the husky coat?

In general, this breed does not need complex care. Husky shed 1-2 times a year, at this time, you should comb the dog every day. All the rest of the time, it is enough to comb the dog once a week.

Does husky get along with other dogs?

Husky doesn't fight with other dogs, especially if it is correctly socialized. However, small animals can cause a hunting instinct in a husky. Keep this in mind if you have other pets at home, like cats or rabbits.

Is husky a good guard dog, since it looks like a wolf?

Husky is no more a relative of the wolf than any other dog breed. Huskies are very friendly to strangers, keeping them as guard dogs is pointless. Aggressive behavior is uncharacteristic of them.
